I am a fourth-year PhD student in Psychology at Princeton University. I work with [Dr. Casey Lew-Williams](https://psych.princeton.edu/person/casey-lew-williams) in the [Princeton Baby Lab](https://babylab.princeton.edu/). Previously, I worked with [Dr. Marisa Casillas](https://humdev.uchicago.edu/directory/marisa-casillas) as the manager of the [Chatter Lab](https://chatterlab.uchicago.edu/) at the University of Chicago. Broadly, my research examines how young children's rich and highly variable everyday language input shapes their early word representations.
